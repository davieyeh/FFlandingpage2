Here at Fantasy Fields, our children, our families, our stories and creating memories are what we’re all about! We are a second-generation family business based in Long Island, NY, and we’re looking for local families to share their family memories.

We would love to hear the stories that make your family unique – and to share them so that others’ can learn from your experiences, laugh as you share your funniest moments, or be inspired by your challenges. From the smallest, everyday moments, like how your little one’s smile can light up the room, to the major events that have given you a new perspective on life, every story is meaningful.

If you have a family story you would like to share, please send a message to our inbox. If your family is selected, you will be invited to come to our studio to create a recording of your story, and to meet the Fantasy Fields family!

Details:

📍Location: Our Bay Shore, Long Island location

📅Date: July 8th & 9th (Saturday and Sunday) 2017

🕐Time: To be determined

🎁Compensation: a selection from our unique, hand-crafted pieces for your little one’s room. You will also receive family photos and some footage from the shoot.

If interested, please inbox us before June 25th (23:59, NY Time) for more details!
